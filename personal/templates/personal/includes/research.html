NA61/SHINE collaboration at CERN


The NA61/SHINE's primary goal is to investigate the phase transitions present in the theory of QCD by means of studying heavy ion collisions at ultra-relativistic energies.
My work concentrates on analysis of the spectra of hadrons produced in collisions of Argon and Scandium ions.
My activities for the experiment were partially supported by National Science Center of Poland - HARMONIA grants (2012/04/M/ST2/00816, 2015/18/M/ST2/00125).

Dose Estimation Analysis


A statistical analysis framework designed to estimate the radiation dose based on observed data. Serves as a data container, allows for function fitting and calculates dose estimates along with statistical errors.
